About Our Organisation

UNITY VISION FOUNDATION is a non-governmental organization based in Navi-Mumbai founded in the year 2023.Our project,is a heartfelt endeavor led by a group of dedicated students who are passionate about improving access to quality education for underprivilleged children in out community.In collaboration with local educational- focused NGO

Our Project,is a collaborative effort between our group of passionate students and the local environmental NGO.The project aims to tackle the pressing issue of child welfare,women empowerment,medical health,and providing scholarships to children.

FUNDRAISING DRIVE: Launch a comprehensive fundraising campaign to collect donations from individuals, businesses, and our school community to support Bright Futures Foundation's educational initiatives.

SCHOLARSHIPS FOR UNDERPRIVILEGED STUDENTS: Use the funds raised to provide scholarships to deserving underprivileged students, covering their tuition fees, books, and educational materials.

LEARNING RESOURSES: Purchase and donate educational resources such as books, computers, and stationery to schools in need.

AWARNESS CAMPAIGN: Raise awareness about the importance of education and the impact of our fundraising efforts through social media, school events, and community outreach.

Organisational structure

UNITY VISION FOUNDATION believes in multiplying its efforts through collaboration & partnerships that bring together government, non-government, and corporate in a collaborative process for socio-economic development, providind scholarships, environment, health, and women empowerment.

Vision

We aim to create a development revolution for the marginalized and socio-economically weaker sections of society.

Mission

Our mission is the creation of such a society where nobody is deprived of the main current of development. We are determined for the establishment of that society in which everybody possesses ‘Human rights’ with dignity.
